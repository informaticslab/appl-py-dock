App Lab is a tool for the Informatics Innovation Unit (IIU), CDC, and its public health partners to test and collaborate on innovative mobile applications and prototypes. App Lab features apps for iOS and Android.

To access IIU mobile prototypes/apps (App Lab), you must agree with the following statements:

  • I acknowledge that I am a member of the CDC developer and/or testing community.
  • I understand that App Lab is meant for CDC staff, authorized agents of CDC (e.g., contractors), or CDC community partners only.
  • I understand that App Lab does NOT contain production apps, but only prototype and/or proof-of-concept apps for collaboration and/or testing purposes.
  • I understand that under non circumstance may I use data that contains personally identifiable information (PII)
